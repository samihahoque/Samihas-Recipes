Samiha's Quick-n-Easy Butter Chicken Recipe!



No. of Servings Prep Time Cook Time
4-5 servings 20 minutes 30 minutes


Ingredients


  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

  • 1 tablespoon butter

  • 1 medium onion diced

  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste

  • 2-3 cloves of finely minced garlic

  • 1 Â½ pounds of chicken cut into 1-inch chunks

  • 4 tablespoons tomato paste

  • 1 tablespoon of garam masala

  • 1 teaspoon of paprika

  • 1 teaspoon Fenugreek powder

  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or yogurt

  • Hot cooked rice or naan for serving


Directions


  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the oil, butter, and onions. Cook the onions down until lightly golden for about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Add the ginger and garlic. Let it cook for about 2-3 minutes until fragrant.

  3. Add the chicken, tomato paste, fenugreek powder, and all the spices. Cook for 6-8 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked through.

  4. Add the heavy cream/yogurt and simmer for 8-9 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. Serve over basmati rice or naan.
