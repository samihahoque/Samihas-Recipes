Samiha's Easy Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe!



No. of Servings Prep Time Cook Time
3-4 servings 15 minutes 10 minutes


Ingredients


  • 1 cup butter, softened

  • 1 cup white sugar

  • 1 cup packed brown sugar

  • 2 eggs

  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

  • 2 teaspoons hot water

  • Â½ teaspoon salt

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour

  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

  • 1 cup chopped nuts (any kind)


Directions


  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.

  2. In a large bowl, combine together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.

  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.

  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.

  5. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and nuts into the batter.

  6. Take large spoonfuls of the cookie dough and shape it into circles. Put onto the prepared cookie sheets.

  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
