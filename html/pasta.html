Samiha's Chicken Meatballs Pasta Recipe!



No. of Servings Prep Time Cook Time
4-5 servings 15 minutes 20 minutes


Ingredients


  • 12 ounces linguine pasta

  • 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara pasta sauce

  • ½ cup breadcrumbs

  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder

  • ½ teaspoon italian seasoning

  • ¼ teaspoon salt

  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper powder

  • 1 pound ground chicken

  • 2 eggs

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil


Directions


  1. In a small bowl, stir together the breadcrumbs, garlic powder, italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.

  2. Add the seasoned breadcrumb mixture to the ground chicken and eggs in a large bowl. Use your hands to combine the ingredients until evenly mixed.

  3. Shape the meat mixture into round meatballs. You should get around 20-25 meatballs.

  4. Add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Once the pan is hot, add the meatballs and let it cook for about one minute on each side, until all sides are browned.

  5. Transfer the cooked meatballs to a clean plate.

  6. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and then add in the pasta. Cook the pasta until tender (7-10 minutes).

  7. Drain the cooked pasta using a large strainer.

  8. Add the marinara pasta sauce to a medium-sized saucepan and heat it over a medium-low temperature.

  9. Add the cooked pasta to the saucepan and stir to combine.

  10. Add the cooked meatballs to the saucepan and stir to coat them in the pasta sauce.

  11. Let the pasta and meatballs simmer in the sauce for 5 minutes. Its now ready to be served!
