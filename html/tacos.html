Samiha's Ground Beef Tacos Recipe!



No. of Servings Prep Time Cook Time
5-6 servings 25 minutes 35 minutes


Ingredients


  • 1 onion

  • 1 red pepper and 1 green pepper

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

  • 1 pound of ground beef

  • 2 cloves of garlic

  • 1 teaspoon of paprika powder

  • 1½ teaspoons cumin

  • 250 ml beef stock

  • 12 taco shells (soft/hard)

    • SALSA


  • 2 large tomatoes

  • 1 small white onion

  • Fresh coriander to preference

  • Juice of ½ a lime

  • 2 tablespoons of sour cream


Directions


  1. Dice the onion and the bell peppers. Cook them in a large pan with 1 tablespoon of olive oil over a low heat until they soften.

  2. Finely chop the garlic and add it to the pan, along with the paprika and cumin powder. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes.

  3. Add the ground beef and stir until it has browned.

  4. Pour in the beef stock, cover, and cook for 45 minutes.

  5. For making the salsa, roughly chop the tomatoes and finely dice the onion.

  6. Roughly chop the coriander leaves. Combine with the lime juice and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C / 350 degrees farenheit

  8. Spread the taco shells out on a baking tray and place in the oven for 3 to 4 minutes until crisp.

  9. Fill the taco shells with the beef mixture and top with the salsa and sour cream.
